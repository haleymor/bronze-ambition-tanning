Tanning Beds & Leg Tanners

With 9 different types of UV tanning units, we at Bronze Ambition pride ourselves on being able to offer our clients a tanning experience that can be individually tailored to best achieve your tanning goals along with complimenting any budget.

Divided into ‘Levels’ that reflect both energy output and comfort, see below to explore our equipment that range from our flagship Level 5 Prestige 1100 to our no frills but exceptionally economical Level 1 ETS Envy 142. Many of our beds offer both variable intensity fans and facial lamps, along with a complementary bottle of water.

Level 5

EROGLINE “PRESTIGE 1100”
  • A new addition to our salon brought in to satisfy clients looking for the very best experience the tanning industry has to offer, the Erogline Prestige 1100 is one of the most powerful and technologically advance tanning beds currently on the market.
  • Featuring 48 x 200-watt top-of-the-line Cosmedico body lamps, 4 x 500-watt facial tanners, and 2 x 240-watt high-pressure shoulder/neck tanners, these strategically positioned lamps provide users with flawlessly deep tan.
  • This bed is perfect for those looking to develop and maintain a deep, dark tan while enjoying creature comforts such as climate control, a body hugging contoured acrylic, AUX music option, aroma therapy and mist.
  • The maximum session time in this state-of-the-art bed is 10 minutes.
  • Best suited to individuals who want to FAST RESULTS and THE MOST COMFORTABLE TANNING EXPERIENCE THE INDUSTRY HAS TO OFFER.

Level 4

SUN CAPSULE “STARSHIP”
  • Not only is the Sun Capsule Starship the most powerful stand-up tanning booth we have to offer, it is arguably our most powerful tanning unit.
  • While this unit does not offer high-pressure facials lamps known for providing instant colour to your face/shoulders, it more than makes up for it by offering 50 x 220-watt top-of-line Cosmedico body lamps, the most powerful body lamps on the market.
  • This booth is perfect for clients who are more comfortable standing and are looking for quick and effective full-body results while being less concerned about developing dramatic colour on their face/neck/shoulders.
  • The maximum session time for this booth is 9 minutes.
  • Best suited to individuals who want FAST RESULTS with a focus on building up their base tan.

Level 4

Dr. MULLER “SUNGATE”
  • Universally considered to be irreplaceable, the Dr. Muller Sungate belongs to a discontinued line of German made tanning units known for their spaciousness and unparalleled tanning performance.
  • While lacking many of the comfort and aesthetics of our Level 5 bed, the Sungate offers the same 200-watt top-of-line Cosmedico body lamps and a whooping 4000 watts of high-pressure UVA light delivered by 4 x 1000-watt facials that brown existing melanin, delivering instantaneous results.
  • This bed is cost effective ‘no frills’ alternative for those looking to develop and maintain a deep dark tan with dramatic colour on their face/neck/shoulders.
  • The maximum session time in this high-performance bed is 12 minutes.
  • Best suited to individuals who want to FAST RESULTS and DRAMATIC RESULTS ON THEIR FACE. Also our most SPACIOUS AND OPEN bed, where client size/inhibitions are a factor

Level 3

Dr. MULLER “ORBIT”
  • By far our most popular bed, the Dr. Muller Orbit, like it’s Level 4 big brother the Sungate, is part of a discontinued line of German made tanning units that offer un-paralleled performance and long have been considered irreplaceable.
  • This ‘no frills’ level 3 tanning option offers the same spectacular 4000 watts of high-pressure facial intensity as the Sungate complimented by top-of-the-line 160-watt Cosmedico body lamps.
  • This bed best suits clients looking for dramatic and instantaneous results on their face/neck/shoulders.
  • The maximum session time in this bed is 12 minutes.
  • Best level 3 option suited to individuals who want DRAMATIC RESULTS ON THEIR FACE.

Level 3

ETS “LEGEND” 442
  • The ETS Legend 442 offers 42 x 160-watt top-of-the-line Cosmedico body lamps and 4 x 520-watt high-pressure facial lamps combined with a comfort features typically reserved for higher performance beds.
  • Features include an AUX/MP3 or Zen music option along with a contoured and frosted acrylic, providing a comfortable tanning platform that diffuses light energy guaranteeing an even tan.
  • This bed is ideally suited to those looking for a cost-effective but comfortable alternative to our higher level tanning options.
  • The maximum session time for this bed is 12 minutes.
  • Best level 3 option suited to individuals who want more economical options to the Level 5 Prestige while still enjoying a range of comfort features.

Level 2

ETS “EPIC” 242
  • The ETS EPIC 242 offers a gentle tan with 42 x 100-watt top-of-the-line Cosmedico body lamps and 3 x 520-watt high-pressure facial lamps combined with a comfort features typically reserved for higher performance beds.
  • Features include an AUX/MP3 or Zen music option along with a contoured and frosted acrylic, providing a comfortable tanning platform that diffuses light energy guaranteeing an even tan.
  • Ideally suited to those looking for a gentle and comfortable alternative to our higher level tanning options.
  • The maximum session time for this bed is 15 minutes.
  • Ideally suited to those looking for a gentle and comfortable alternative to our higher level tanning options.

Level 1

ETS “ENVY” 142
  • The ETS ENVY 142 is our most gentle and cost-effective tanning option. As a step above tanning units found at chain tanning salons and gyms, the ENVY is equipped with 34 x 100-watt body lamps complimented by 3 x 520- watt high-pressure facials and a frosted acrylic to diffuse the light energy, delivering consistent and even results.
  • The ENVY is best suited to budget conscious clients and those who prefer to savour their tanning experience.
  • The maximum session time for this bed is 20 minutes.
  • Best suited to BUDGET CONSCIOUS clients who don’t mind taking the time to savour their tanning experience.

Classic Leg Tanner

"Classic Legs"
  • In a league of its own, our ‘Classic Legs’ leg tanner allows our clients to focus on those hard to tan legs, surrounding them with 38 x 65-watt body lamps. Most popular in the spring, this unit is the solution to anyone wanting to showcase perfectly tanned legs.
  • This unit is often used either before or after a session in one of our other UV beds, allowing you to make the most of your visit.
